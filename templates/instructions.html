Game
Overview

Be in control of the game!!!

Players move around the game board collecting treasures worth points.

You will have to choose the dimensions of the board and how many players will play.

Players take turns to move, rest, or attack and each player starts with a set amount of energy and 0 points.

Players use energy to move around the board, collect treasures, and attack other players.

Energy is consumed when a player moves. The amount of energy used depends on the distance moved.

Players can rest to regain energy.

Players if they collect weapons, can decide to attack other players.

Weapons
& Treasures

Master the Game!!!

The game ends when all treasures are collected or only one player remains and the player with the most points wins the game. If only one player is left, they automatically win regardless of points.

Players can be eliminated by being attacked or by another player moving onto their cell. When attacking, a player uses a collected weapon. The weapon is consumed after use.

Treasures:

Silver: 20 points

Gold: 25 points

Emerald: 35 points

Diamond: 40 points

Platinum: 50 points

Weapons:

Gun: 5 strike units

Grenade: 3 strike untis
